New Rook Game

Tolman Rules from rookgame.com — full 57-card deck, four players, two partnerships, 5-card kitty.

Tolman Rules

  • 1 is high in each suit (above 14). Rook is the lowest trump in the trump color.
  • Counters: 1 = 15, 14 = 10, 10 = 10, 5 = 5, Rook = 20 — 180 per hand.
  • Kitty: winner of the last trick scores counters left in the kitty.
  • Sweep: take all 180 counters in tricks for +20 bonus (200 total).
  • Bid/set: make your bid or lose it; the other team always keeps their counters. Play to 500.

Game length

Tolman Rules play to 500; hand limit is optional if you stop early.
